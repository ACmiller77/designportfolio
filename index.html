Award-winning design

The Society for News Design honored me and my design collaborator, Erin Hebert, with an Award of Excellence in 2019 for these three front pages at the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette. The pages will be featured in the 40th edition of the SND's annual, "The Best of News Design." The Post-Gazette's reporting on the mass shooting at Tree of Life synagogue won the 2019 Pulitzer Prize for Breaking News. In my career as a news designer, my work has been honored with awards from The Florida Press Club, the Pennsylvania Newspaper Association, the Tennessee Press Association and the Press Club of Western Pennsylvania. In 2015, Gatehouse Media named me Designer of the Year.

Digital design

I've put my skills to work on the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette's mobile and tablet app, NewsSlide. It's an innovative format for presenting news—strong on visuals and designed for easy navigation on touch-screen devices.

Print design

My career as a news designer has taken me to the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, Chattanooga Times Free Press, Daytona Beach News-Journal and the Hawaii Tribune-Herald. My work has been featured in Poynter's Front Pages of the Day and the Newseum's Top Ten Pages of the Day.

Web design

I coded the sites below in HTML5 and CSS3 as projects for a front-end development bootcamp program. I structured both sites with Bootstrap's grid system, and the G.I. Haul site is an example of responsive design. It features Bootstrap modals that run on javascript.
