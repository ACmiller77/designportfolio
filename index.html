Tools of my trade

Award-winning design

The Society for News Design honored me and my design collaborator, Erin Hebert, with an Award of Excellence in 2019 for these three front pages at the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette. The pages will be featured in the 40th edition of the SND's annual, "The Best of News Design." The Post-Gazette's reporting on the mass shooting at Tree of Life synagogue won the 2019 Pulitzer Prize for Breaking News. In my career as a news designer, my work has been honored with awards from The Florida Press Club, the Pennsylvania Newspaper Association and the Press Club of Western Pennsylvania. In 2015, Gatehouse Media named me Designer of the Year.

Print design

My career as a news designer has taken me to the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, Chattanooga Times Free-Press, Daytona Beach News-Journal and the Hawaii Tribune Herald. Pages I've designed have been featured in Poynter's Frontpages of the Day and the Newseum's Top Ten Pages of the Day.
